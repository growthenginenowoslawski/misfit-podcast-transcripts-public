# Show-Level Summary

This repository covers the last 6 months of **Misfit Podcast** by **Misfit Athletics**: 27 episodes from 2025-11-18 through 2026-05-05. The repo is designed for ChatGPT users to answer questions by reading summaries and topic indexes first, then opening transcript chunks only when exact wording or timestamp evidence is needed.

## Core Misfit Themes

- **Phase-based training beats random workouts.** The show repeatedly frames progress through structured phases, blocks, and season context rather than novelty.
- **The competitive ladder matters.** Open, Quarterfinals, Semifinals, and Games prep are treated as different jobs with different expectations.
- **Weaknesses are the best map for progress.** Post-competition debriefs, accessory work, gymnastics, and aerobic base all show up as ways to turn exposed limitations into training direction.
- **Athlete IQ is a performance skill.** Pacing, stimulus selection, self-awareness, strategy, and in-workout adjustment are treated as trainable capacities.
- **Training, testing, and competing are distinct.** The show warns against confusing everyday training with proof-of-fitness moments.
- **Recovery and dose management are part of the program.** Competition overload, Zone 2, nutrition, and long-term consistency all appear as performance infrastructure.
- **Coaching communication matters.** Programming only works when athletes understand the why, buy into the phase, and can apply it under pressure.
- **Substance over image.** The Misfit Mind episodes especially push away from external validation and toward long-term, internally owned improvement.

## Transcript Quality

Transcripts are automatic outputs from `mlx-community/whisper-small.en-mlx`. They are useful for search, summaries, and timestamped evidence, but exact quotes should be checked against neighboring chunks and, when high stakes, against the source audio. Speaker labels are not included in v1.
