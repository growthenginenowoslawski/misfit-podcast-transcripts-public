- They define what accessory work actually is (and what it isn’t), then go deep on how Misfit programs it, how it should be executed, and why most athletes either misuse it or skip it entirely.
- secondary accessory structure to the concept of “runway,” this episode lays out a clear framework for building strength, durability, and long-term progress without interfering with conditioning.

- If you’ve ever wondered how to actually use accessory work to get better at CrossFit, this is your guide.
